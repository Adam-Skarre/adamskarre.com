Electromechanical Systems Modeling

Thermal performance modeling for an electrical enclosure: CAD parameterization, CFD-based natural convection analysis, and design iteration to improve temperature behavior across operating constraints.

ABB Senior Design Siemens NX ANSYS Fluent Natural Convection DoE / Iteration

What I Did

  • Built a parameter-driven enclosure model and controlled geometry changes (louvers, cutouts, thickness, etc.).
  • Set up CFD workflows to estimate temperature rise and airflow patterns under natural convection.
  • Ran iterative comparisons across design variants and operating assumptions to support decision-making.
  • Packaged results into presentation-ready outputs (figures, tables, and narrative for stakeholders).
