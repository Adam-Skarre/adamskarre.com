Airplane CAD Model

WWII-style aircraft modeled for aerodynamic design, SolidWorks surface features, and assembly planning.

Project Details

Constructed fuselage and wings using Siemens NX surface modeling techniques with a focus on aerodynamic performance. Digitized and positioned cross-sectional curves based on historical aircraft data. Created watertight surfaces using Through Curve, Bridge Surface, and Edge Blend tools.

  • Performed Gaussian and highlight line curvature analysis to ensure flow continuity
  • Final model exhibited zero inflection points and verified aerodynamic surface quality

Drone Assembly

Modular drone model with detailed part files including propeller, drive gear, shafts, and full mechanical layout.

Miscellaneous CAD Models

Standalone components created for precision modeling and detail exploration in SolidWorks and NX.

Project Details

These models were created to explore mechanical component design and assembly fit. Each part was dimensioned to typical industry standards using SolidWorks and Siemens NX.
